## Challenge

--- task ---
Add an **LED** status indicator on **GP13** that lights when the tap is on.
--- /task ---

--- task ---
Add a **buzzer** to sound if the valve stays open for more than 15 seconds. This will help detect possible sensor errors or water wastage.
--- /task ---

--- task ---
Add a **day/night mode** using a photoresistor or by checking the time, so that the tap system powers down at night to save energy.
--- /task ---

--- task ---
Add a **flow sensor** to count the litres of water dispensed and print the total volume to the Shell every minute. 

You can expand this later to log the data to a CSV file to help you track water use.
--- /task ---

--- task ---
Connect to an **IoT service** to upload valve activity or flow data to use for logging and analysis.
--- /task ---
